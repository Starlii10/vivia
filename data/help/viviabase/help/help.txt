# ViviaBase: `help`

The help extension adds a command that lets you open help messages like these!

## Command
Usage: `v!help <extension>`
Examples:
* `v!help`
* `v!help viviabase.help`
* `v!help customextension`

Not every extension has one, so you may have to experiment with commands to find out what they do.
